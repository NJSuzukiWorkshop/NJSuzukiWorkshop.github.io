Every Child Can!© An Introduction to Suzuki Education with Nancy Modell

For teachers and parents: We are excited to again offer Every Child Can!© course. Nancy Modell will teach the Every Child Can! course on Friday, July 7 from 10:00am-5:00pm (registration at 9:30, includes a lunch break). This course can be registered with the SAA. After the ECC course is complete, please join us Friday evening for our 7:00 play-in!

Every Child Can!© is a one-day introduction to the Suzuki philosophy and its application to education. This class is designed for parents, teachers, prospective teachers, and other interested adults. The course provides an inspiring, in-depth look at the Suzuki approach to teaching and learning. For teachers, ECC serves as the first course in the Suzuki Association of the Americas Teacher Development program.

The Lawrenceville School 2500 Main Street Lawrenceville, NJ
