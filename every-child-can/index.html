Joan Krzywicki, Piano, Every Child Can!© Course

Joan Krzywicki earned a Bachelor of Music Education Degree from Indiana University in Bloomington, IN, where she carried a second major in Piano Performance. She studied piano there with Abbey Simon. She also has a Master's Degree from Youngstown State University in Ohio. Joan became an official Teacher Trainer for the Suzuki Association of the Americas in 1993. She maintains a private studio in her home and offers teacher training at Temple University in Philadelphia. Joan is also nationally certified by the Music Teachers National Association.

She has been a guest clinician and teacher trainer at workshops and Institutes in the U.S., Canada, England, Sweden, and Argentina. Joan is serving as Chair of the Board of Directors of the Suzuki Association of the Americas. She is also a Past President of the Philadelphia Music Teachers Association and the Greater Philadelphia Suzuki Association.

Every Child Can!© An Introduction to the Suzuki Approach with Joan Krzywicki

Joan Krzywicki

For teachers and parents: We are excited that you may begin your Suzuki training journey or review the foundations of the Suzuki Philosophy with us.

After completing ECC!©, join us on Sunday to observe our clinicians teach violin, viola, cello, and piano. Come and join in the fun!

Every Child Can!© is a one-day introduction to the Suzuki philosophy and its application to education. This class is designed for teachers, prospective teachers, and other interested adults. The course provides an inspiring, in-depth look at the Suzuki approach to teaching and learning. For teachers, ECC!© serves as the first course in the Suzuki Association of the Americas Teacher Development program.
