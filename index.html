New Jersey Suzuki Workshop

July 7-8, 2018

Welcome

Join us on Juy 7-8, 2018, for our 10th annual NJ Suzuki Workshop, held on the beautiful Lawrenceville School campus. Immerse yourself in a weekend of music-making with Suzuki students and teachers! The Suzuki Workshop includes a core curriculum for pianists, cellists, violists, and violinists in Suzuki Books 1–6, a Pre-Twinkle violin class, and the Every Child Can!© course for teachers and parents. We’ll offer master classes, group classes, and Dalcroze music and movement with our renowned clinicians throughout the weekend, and end on Sunday with a concert for all participants. Summer is a perfect time of the year for musical motivation! Come enjoy a quality Suzuki weekend in your own backyard. Make new friends while sharing the joy of making music!

Early Bird Registration open now through April 30, 2018. Please browse through our new website for important information. We look forward to seeing you this summer!
