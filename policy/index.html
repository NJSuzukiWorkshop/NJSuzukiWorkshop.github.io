Workshop Policy and Information

We strive to make this workshop a wonderful experience for all who are involved. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions regarding our policies. Enjoy the weekend!

Parent Responsibilities

Parents (or guardians) are required to attend the entire workshop with their children. Parents are exclusively responsible for their children and all belongings. In addition, parents are expected to take notes and/or video in order to follow up in home practice, and may be asked to participate in classes.

Absences and Refunds

Refunds will not be given for missed classes or days at the workshop. NJ Suzuki Workshop reserves the right to cancel any portion of the workshop due to insufficient enrollment. Families affected will receive a refund and a minimum of two weeks notice.

Cancellations after June 1

Will receive a refund of $100 if another student can be placed from a waiting list. ECC registrants who cancel after June 1 will receive a refund of $75 if another participant can be placed from a waiting list. There are no refunds for the Pre-Twinkle Class after June 1.

Registrations after July 1

We may be able to accept late applications, however, we cannot guarantee students will receive a masterclass or chamber group if registering after July 1. The workshop fee will remain the same.

Photography

Photos will be taken for use in future promotional materials for the workshop. Names will not be associated with photos. We welcome any photos you take and would like to share from the workshop! Please email them to njsuzukiworkshop@yahoo.com.

Buildings and Grounds

We will strictly enforce the Lawrenceville School policies. Only park in designated parking lots, use the buildings we are given access to, and please help take care of the spaces we occupy.
