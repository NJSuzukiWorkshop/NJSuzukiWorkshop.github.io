Workshop Policy and Information

Please read through the below carefully. Do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions regarding our policies.

Parent Responsibilities

Parents (or guardians) are required to attend the entire workshop with their children. Parents are exclusively responsible for their children and all belongings. Parents are expected to give their full attention in classes and be willing to participate.

Discounts and Scholarships

Early Bird discount

Registrants will receive a $10 discount, one per family.

Sibling discount

Siblings (not including the first registrant) will receive a $20 discount per child.

Scholarship applications

Must be received by May 1 and families will be notified by email by May 15. Scholarships will be in the form of a registration discount.

Absences and Refunds

Refunds will not be given for missed classes or days at the workshop. NJ Suzuki Workshop (NJSW) reserves the right to cancel any portion of the workshop due to insufficient enrollment. In this case, families affected will receive refunds for that portion of the workshop and a minimum of two weeks’ notice.

Cancellations from June 1-June 30

Will receive a refund of $100 if another student can be placed from a waiting list. Every Child Can! (ECC) registrants who cancel after June 1 will receive a refund of $75 if another participant can be placed from a waiting list. There are no refunds for the Pre-Twinkle Class after June 1.

Cancellations after July 1

No refunds given after this date. Registrations may still be accepted (including for ECC and Pre-Twinkle), however, we cannot guarantee students will receive a masterclass.

Photography

Photos will be taken for use in future promotional materials for the workshop. Children’s and parents’ names will not be associated with photos.

Buildings and Grounds

We will strictly enforce The College Of New Jersey’s policies. Only park in designated lots, use only the spaces we are given access to, and please help take care of the spaces we occupy.
