New Jersey Suzuki Workshop

About

Immerse yourself in a weekend of music-making with Suzuki students and teachers! The Suzuki Workshop includes a core curriculum for pianists, cellists, violists, and violinists in Suzuki Books 1–6, a Pre-Twinkle violin class, and the Every Child Can!© course for teachers and parents. We’ll kick off the weekend with a play-in on Friday evening, offer master classes, group classes, and enrichment classes with our renowned clinicians throughout the weekend, and end on Sunday with a concert for all participants. Summer is a perfect time of the year for musical motivation! Come enjoy a quality Suzuki weekend in your own backyard. Make new friends while sharing the joy of making music!
