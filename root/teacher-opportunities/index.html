Teacher Opportunities

Observe master and group classes led by our violin clinicians, our piano clinician, enrichment classes, and our parent talk. We encourage teachers to volunteer by assisting with such things as set-up, registration, tuning, monitoring group classes, leading at play-ins, taking photos, and cleanup. For all teachers who volunteer at least two hours, observations are free of charge and lunch will be provided! For teachers who are not able to volunteer, you may still observe for a fee of $10. This is a great opportunity to be involved in our Suzuki community and to spend time with our fellow teachers. Please fill out the volunteer form below. Thank you for your participation!
