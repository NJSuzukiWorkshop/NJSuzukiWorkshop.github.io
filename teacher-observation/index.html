Teacher Opportunities

Teachers, please RSVP for the Workshop here.

This weekend summer workshop is a great opportunity to be involved in our Suzuki community and to spend time with your fellow teachers.

Enhance your teaching by observing our master and group classes led by strings and piano clinicians, and enrichment (music and movement) classes. Gain professional development with our special Piano Teacher Event.

For teachers who volunteer hands-on at the Workshop, observations are free of charge and lunch will be provided. For teachers who wish to observe only, the fee of $10 covers the whole weekend.

Register Online

Volunteer responses requested by June 1.

The teacher observation fee will be waived for:

  • GPSA (Greater Philadelphia Suzuki Association) member teachers who send one or more students to the Workshop
  • All undergraduate students
  • Rowan and TCNJ graduate students
  • Keys2Success piano staff
