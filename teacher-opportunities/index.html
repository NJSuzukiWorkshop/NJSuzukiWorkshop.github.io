Teacher Opportunities

Enhance your teaching by observing our master and group classes led by strings and piano clinicians, and enrichment (music and movement) classes. Gain professional development with our Every Child Can!© class.

We encourage area teachers to volunteer to assist with set-up, registration, tuning, monitoring group classes, leading at play-ins, taking photos, and cleanup. For all teachers who volunteer two hours, observations are free of charge and lunch will be provided! For teachers who are not able to volunteer, you may still observe for a fee of $10.

This is a great opportunity to be involved in our Suzuki community and to spend time with your fellow teachers. To volunteer, please fill out this form. Thank you for your participation!

Responses requested by mid-June.
