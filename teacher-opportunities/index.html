Teacher Opportunities

Teachers, please RSVP for the Workshop here.

This weekend summer workshop is a great opportunity to be involved in our Suzuki community and to spend time with your fellow teachers.

Enhance your teaching by observing our master and group classes led by strings and piano clinicians, and enrichment (music and movement) classes. Gain professional development with our Every Child Can!© and our Rolland Teacher Training classes.

We encourage area teachers to volunteer to assist with set-up, ushering families, tuning, monitoring group classes, leading at play-ins, and clean-up. For teachers who volunteer two hours, observations are free of charge and lunch will be provided. For teachers who wish to observe only, the fee of $10 covers the whole weekend.

Responses requested by mid-June.
